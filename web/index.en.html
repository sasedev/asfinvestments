About us

ASF INVESTMENTS SA is a Swiss wealth management company that specialises in international clienteles.

Our worldwide presence and our international expertise enable us to help you establish long-term tax and investment strategies.

Our mission is to advise clients and manage their assets in a completely independent manner.

Whether you are an entrepreneur, an executive or you represent a legal entity, our mission is to assist you within a privileged relationship, one that is based on trust and the long-term, in order to meet all of your estate needs.

Consulting and Structuring

Our main mission here at ASF INVESTMENTS SA is to assess and advise you in terms of the legal and fiscal structure of your private and business assets.

In fact, whether you are a private investor or an entrepreneur, an examination of your estate in order to assess your situation and propose strategies that meet your goals is an essential prerequisite.

As today's legal and tax environment is constantly changing, we ensure that the legal, tax and estate strategies we establish are appropriate.

Our lawyers and our tax experts will subsequently accompany you in the implementation of our recommendations and your decisions.

Asset management via multi-management: A customised service

Our investment philosophy is based on a secure and totally independent approach. Although our goal is to ensure performance, the priority is the preservation of your capital.

ASF INVESTMENTS SA's expertise entails an objective quantitative analysis combined with a qualitative analysis in order to select and combine the funds and managers that are most appropriate for the market environment.

Many competitors claim to have the same principle, paradoxically developing their own range of products which are specifically aimed at their own clients. This is obviously not the case with us.

We offer two types of services:

  • Management mandate

Clients who wish to delegate the management of their portfolio may entrust us with a management mandate. Together, we will then define an orientation that meets their objectives as well as the risk level that is best for them. We constantly adapt our management choices and our asset allocations according to the economic situation and financial market developments.

  • Investment consulting

Clients who wish to actively manage their own investments can be accompanied in their choices through regular input in the form of personalised recommendations that are tailored to their convictions.

Fiduciary & trustee services

The establishment of a trust or foundation can allow you to secure your estate and plan its transfer to future generations while optimising your tax situation.

In addition, a trust or foundation can, for example, allow you to decide in advance who will look after your assets in the event that you are unable to do so in the future. This will ensure that your loved ones will not have to worry about your daily business affairs instead of your well-being.

We can also assist you in the preparation of your will.

Our role will be to oversee the protection of your assets, ensure the proper handling of your accounts and provide your beneficiaries with detailed reports.

It is therefore important that you appoint a trustee with experience, one that is known for its impartiality and its ability to administer assets, particularly in order to enhance your wealth according to your goals.
